Location Details - The Light’s Dominion

Location Description Characters Monsters
Lightfort Elyria’s bastion, a fortress of light standing against the darkness, but corrupted by zealotry. Sister Lirien, Blinded Saint: A devout follower of Elyria, purging darkness with divine light. Zealot Constructs: Animated statues imbued with Elyria’s light, attacking heretics.
Valley of Illusions A mystical rift bleeding from the Mirror’s power, where reality bends and mirages deceive. Thryme, Backward Prophet: A seer lost in the valley, seeing the past as the future. Illusionary Phantoms: Mirages that take form to confuse and attack intruders.
Mountains of Madness Zarkan’s tomb, a chaotic range of jagged peaks where madness seeps from the earth. Zarath, Shard Keeper: A mad guardian of a Mirror shard, driven insane by its power. Chaos Beasts: Monstrous creatures born of Zarkan’s chaotic energy, roaming the peaks.
Creststone City Torval’s stronghold, a grand city of light and oppression, where the faithful rule with an iron fist. Veyra, Bone Singer: Leader of a death cult, weaving necromantic songs to serve Torval. Necrotic Guardians: Undead soldiers raised by Veyra to protect the city.
Avalon The northeastern capital of Eyloria, seat of Mortimer II, where the Mirror of Worlds shapes reality under the Light’s rule. Mortimer II: Ruler of Avalon, wielder of the Mirror of Worlds, bending reality to his will. Mirror Sentinels: Beings created by the Mirror of Worlds, defending Avalon.
